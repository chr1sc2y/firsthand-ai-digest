Constitutional AI: Harmlessness from AI Feedback
We present Constitutional AI (CAI), a method for training AI systems to be helpful, harmless, and honest using AI-generated feedback instead of human labels for harmlessness. The approach works by first generating responses using a set of principles drawn from a "constitution", then asking the AI to critique and revise its own outputs using that same constitution — a self-supervised process that scales better than relying solely on human annotators.

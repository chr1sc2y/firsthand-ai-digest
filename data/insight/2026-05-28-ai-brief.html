NotebookLM gets automatic Google Drive syncing

Fact: Google says NotebookLM can automatically sync Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides sources from Drive, respect permission revocations, and remove deleted Drive files from notebooks.

Analysis: This reduces stale-context risk for teams using notebooks as living research workspaces. It also makes access governance more important because NotebookLM now mirrors Drive state rather than a one-time upload snapshot.

Sources: Josh Woodward public post, Google Workspace Updates.

curl maintainer pressure from AI-assisted reports

Fact: Daniel Stenberg says curl's security report volume is 4-5x 2024 and about double 2025, with more detailed and credible reports. He also says recent curl vulnerabilities have generally been low or medium severity.

Analysis: AI can improve vulnerability discovery, but the operational load lands on maintainers. Projects need triage funding, validation tooling, and clearer disclosure expectations, not just more reports.

Sources: Daniel Stenberg, Simon Willison link post, curl CVE-2023-38545 reference.

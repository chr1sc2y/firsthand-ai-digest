HIGH

Facts

  • After biometric approval, 1Password injects credentials directly into the page, scoped to the current task. S004
  • The browser extension adds Agentic Mode that locks the extension when an agent takes over, only allowing authorized logins. S004

Uncertainty. The integration is currently Mac-only; Windows and Linux support timelines are unknown.
